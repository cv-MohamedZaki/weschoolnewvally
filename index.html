Information About the School

Applied Technology Schools are a type of technical education institutions that rely on partnerships with private sector companies to prepare skilled and trained technicians and professionals in their specialized fields.
Specializations in Applied Technology Schools vary to meet the changing needs of the job market, aiming to provide students with the skills and knowledge required to work in the fields of technology and innovation industries.

We School of Applied Technology - is the first specialized school in the fields of communications and information technology. It's a partnership between the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT) and Telecom Egypt. The school's educational system is based on the competency-based education approach.

The competency-based education approach is a system that ensures that WE School students possess the fundamental factors to become professional technicians. This approach aims to create skilled and exceptional technicians who excel in their fields of specialization.
The approach includes:
* Theoretical knowledge
* Practical skills
* Ethics and life skills (such as effective communication skills, teamwork, and more)

The admission requirements may vary from year to year, but for the previous year 2024/2025, they were:
* The student must be medically fit
* The student's age should not exceed 18 years on October 1st
* The student must hold Egyptian nationality
* Proficiency in the English language
* The student's total score in the preparatory certificate should not be less than 225 points (this may vary from year to year)
* The student's successful completion of the admission tests and personal interview

* Initially, I want to assure you that the admission tests are not difficult, the questions are not impossible, and you won't be required to solve 200 questions in half an hour. This is very normal and quite okay, especially since you are in the third year of preparatory school. You'll be asked about things you've studied before, so don't worry.
